CrystalsCollector !

The player will be shown a random number at the start of the game.

There will be four crystals displayed as buttons on the page. When the player clicks on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score.

The player wins if their total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game. The player loses if their score goes above the random number.

Your game will hide this amount until the player clicks a crystal.

When the game begins again, the player should see a new random number for each crystal.
